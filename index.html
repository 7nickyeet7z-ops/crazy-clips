Crazy Clips Private Tool

Crazy Clips is a private, local tool that generates short clips and uploads them to the user’s TikTok Inbox as drafts for manual posting.

Overview TikTok API Products & Scopes Data Handling Privacy Policy Terms of Service Contact

What Crazy Clips does

  • Processes user's long-form videos locally to generate short vertical clips.
  • Creates subtitles and a suggested caption for each clip.
  • Uploads each clip as a draft to the user’s TikTok Inbox using the TikTok API.
  • The user reviews/edits and posts inside TikTok (no automatic publishing).

This tool is intended for the account owner’s use and runs on the user’s own machine.

TikTok API Products & scopes used

  • Login Kit: authenticates the user and obtains authorization.
  • Content Posting (Inbox/Draft Upload): uploads videos to the user’s TikTok Inbox as drafts.

Scopes

  • user.info.basic — used to confirm the authenticated account.
  • video.upload — used to upload videos to TikTok Inbox as drafts.

Crazy Clips does not auto-publish content. Posting happens manually inside the TikTok app by the user.

Data handling

  • Authentication tokens are stored locally on the user’s device.
  • Video processing (clip creation/subtitles/captions) happens locally.
  • Uploads send the final clip files to TikTok Inbox via the API.
  • After successful upload, the tool may archive processed files locally (optional).

Crazy Clips does not sell data and is not a consumer-facing service.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-02

1) Who we are

Crazy Clips is a private tool used by its account owner to edit their videos and send to TikTok Inbox.

2) Information we collect

  • TikTok authorization tokens (access/refresh tokens) stored locally on the user’s machine.
  • Local files created during processing (clips, subtitles, caption sidecar text, logs).

3) How we use information

  • To authenticate with TikTok and upload draft videos to the user’s TikTok Inbox.
  • To keep a local processing log and avoid re-processing already completed work.

4) Sharing

Crazy Clips does not share personal information with third parties, except that uploaded media is transmitted to TikTok for the purpose of placing drafts into the user’s Inbox.

5) Storage and security

Tokens and logs are stored locally. The user is responsible for device security and access control.

6) Retention

Processed videos and logs remain on the user’s device until archived or deleted by the user’s settings/workflow.
