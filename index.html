Crazy Clips For Creators

Crazy Clips is a video automation platform for gaming content creators. Creators authenticate with their TikTok account and the platform automatically generates short vertical clips from long-form gaming videos — complete with subtitles and captions — and uploads them as drafts to their TikTok Inbox for review before posting.

What Crazy Clips does

  • Creators connect their TikTok account via OAuth to get started.
  • The platform processes long-form gaming videos to generate short vertical clips.
  • Creates subtitles and a suggested caption for each clip.
  • Uploads each clip as a draft to the creator's TikTok Inbox using the TikTok API.
  • The creator reviews, edits, and posts inside TikTok — no automatic publishing occurs.

TikTok API Products & scopes used

  • Login Kit: authenticates the creator and obtains authorization via OAuth.
  • Content Posting (Inbox/Draft Upload): uploads videos to the creator's TikTok Inbox as drafts.

Scopes

  • user.info.basic — used to confirm the authenticated account.
  • video.upload — used to upload videos to TikTok Inbox as drafts.

Crazy Clips does not auto-publish content. Posting happens manually inside the TikTok app by the creator.

Data handling

  • Authentication tokens are stored locally on the creator's device and never shared.
  • Video processing (clip creation, subtitles, captions) happens locally on the creator's machine.
  • Uploads send the final clip files to the creator's TikTok Inbox via the TikTok API.
  • After successful upload, processed files may be archived locally.

Crazy Clips does not sell or share user data with any third parties.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-02

1) Who we are

Crazy Clips is a video automation platform that helps gaming content creators generate and upload short-form clips to their TikTok accounts.

2) Information we collect

  • TikTok authorization tokens (access/refresh tokens) stored locally on the creator's machine.
  • Local files created during processing (clips, subtitles, caption text, logs).

3) How we use information

  • To authenticate with TikTok on behalf of the creator and upload draft videos to their TikTok Inbox.
  • To maintain a local processing log and avoid re-processing already completed work.

4) Sharing

Crazy Clips does not share personal information with third parties, except that uploaded media is transmitted to TikTok for the purpose of placing drafts into the creator's Inbox.

5) Storage and security

Tokens and logs are stored locally on the creator's device. The creator is responsible for device security and access control.

6) Retention

Processed videos and logs remain on the creator's device until archived or deleted by the creator.
